# Feature: Chart Layouts

## Purpose

To define chart layouts when rendered.

## Requirements

- The chart will be configured using the options dialog.

### Layout: Vertical Month

- The chart is laid out with days displayed in a column for each month, from 1st at the top, and last day at the bottom.
- A heading row at the top will show the month name.
- A heading column at the left will list the days of the week repeated.  The selected first day of the week will be at the top.
- Cell will be aligned with the day of the week on the left.
- The First of the month will be aligned with the uppermost instance of the corresponding day of the week.
